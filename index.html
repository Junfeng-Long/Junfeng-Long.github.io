Hello there! I am a visiting scholar at Hybrid Robotics, UC Berkeley advised by Prof. Koushil Sreenath. Previously, I worked on Legged Robot Locomotion with Dr. Jiangmiao Pang and Distributed Systems with Prof. Youlong Wu. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from ShanghaiTech University.

I am interested in enabling robotic systems to achieve functionality and safety. Toward this goal, I focus on: (a) bridging control and machine learning to develop methods possessing their advantages; (b) training and application of dynamics models (world models); (c) developing robot foundation model for general purpose robots.

I am actively applying for Ph.D. position starting from Fall 2025. If you are interested in my research or want to chat, please drop me an email.

Parallelizing Model-based Reinforcement Learning Over the Sequence Length

Zirui Wang, Yue Deng, Junfeng Long, Yin Zhang†

2024 Annual Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems

[Paper] [Code] [BibTeX]

We propose the Parallelized Model-based Reinforcement Learning (PaMoRL) framework. PaMoRL introduces two novel techniques: the Parallel World Model (PWM) and the Parallelized Eligibility Trace Estimation (PETE) to parallelize both model and policy learning stages of current MBRL methods over the sequence length.
